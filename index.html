About

Continuously generated algorithmic music coded by one human. Inspired by Dadaism, Pratītyasamutpāda, amplituhedron, cut-up and dub. This exists, then that exists. Emergent. Enjoy! SMPLR is the result of a lifetime of questions; on music, the act of creation, artistic legacy, curated chaos, audible reality vs. an extrapolated unknown. SMPLR is both the artist and the generative engine.
